MY JOURNRY IN SKATEBOARDING

WHEN I STARTED SKATEBOARDING


I go by the name of Refilwe Precious Mokhoeya and I am 19 years of age. My hobby is skateboarding.My skateboarding name is Skivvygee.I started skating when it was covid 19 times, which it was 2021. I started skateboarding because the things I used to do before this pandemic ,I couldn’t do them no more.Everything was closed and in a certain time we were supposed to be home. Skateboarding was something that I fell In love with in a snap of my fingers.Skateboarding taught me a lot about life in general but giving lesson through skate language.But at first when I used to see people skate around it did not bother me of I want to skate too.My first time skating I bought a fake board ,which made people laugh but also they helped me get proper materials for my board.They helped me to even start pushing ,doing tricks and helped me get a new set of a board.In skateboarding I have found brothers and sisters.I have a family that is always there for you with out them knowing that they really heal me from the things I cannot talk about.Another thing that made me to join skateboarding was because I was being bullied and having skateboarding as my hobby it changed everything because to me it’s a therapy session when it skating session.It taught me that ,no matter how life it could be never give up ,even how many times you failed.It showed me that nothing is impossible in life and what ever you want you should put your heart into it and you will succeed with flying colours. I could say I am very passionate about it.I started to have a family ,that I never thought I could have in my life.Having to think that being bullied was the thing I got used to and in my being bullied journey I was told that no one will ever love me or even want be friends with me.Looking where I am at now gives me tears because being me was the only thing that people wanted and they really falling in love with me not the fake me.
